About Hone I Fa School

Established in 2016, Hone I Fa is a leading English-medium educational institution in Myanmar, offering a comprehensive curriculum from Kindergarten through Grade 4 in 2024. Our school provides a well-rounded education, with a focus on subjects including English, Burmese, Mathematics, and Social Science. As a testament to our commitment to excellence, we proudly hold a tax business license granted by the Myanmar government in 2020. Hone I Fa is committed to delivering an exceptional learning experience that prepares students for future success, both academically and personally. Through our nurturing environment and holistic approach to education, we empower our students to reach their full potential.

Vision

  • To build an environment that nurtures inquisitive mind and values professionalism
  • To create environment that encourage not only children's self confidence and efficacy but also their harmonious involvement in society in the future

Mission

  • To provide quality pre-school education
  • To encourage children to explore their aptitude by providing caring and supportive environment
  • To pilot teaching and learning resources and establish good practices for sharing with the pre-school sector in Shan States.
