About Hone I Fa School

Founded in 2016, Hone I Fa is an English-medium school in Myanmar offering Nursery to Grade 4. We focus on strong foundations in English, Burmese, Mathematics, Science, and Social Studies in a safe and supportive environment.

The school holds an official tax business license granted by the Myanmar government in 2020, reflecting our commitment to quality and accountability.

Vision

  • To build an environment that nurtures inquisitive minds and values professionalism.
  • To create an environment that encourages children's self-confidence and efficacy and supports their harmonious involvement in society in the future.

Mission

  • To provide quality pre-school and primary education.
  • To encourage children to explore their aptitudes in a caring and supportive environment.
  • To pilot teaching and learning resources and establish good practices for sharing with the pre-school sector in Shan State.
