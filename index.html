About Hone I Fa School

Founded in 2016, Hone I Fa is an English-medium school in Myanmar offering Nursery to Grade 4. We focus on English, Burmese, Mathematics, Science, and Social Studies in a supportive environment.

Official tax business license granted by the Myanmar government in 2020.

Vision

  • To nurture inquisitive minds and professionalism.
  • To build self-confidence and harmonious societal involvement.

Mission

  • To provide quality pre-school and primary education.
  • To help children explore aptitudes in a caring environment.
  • To establish good practices for the pre-school sector in Shan State.
